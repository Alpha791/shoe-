We only collect information that you voluntarily send to us through WhatsApp messages.

We use your WhatsApp number only to:

We do not share, sell, or rent your WhatsApp number or any data you send to us.
Your communication is handled privately and securely through WhatsApp’s end-to-end encryption.

4. Data Security

We respect your privacy and take reasonable steps to keep your information secure.
All communication remains within WhatsApp’s secure system — no data is stored or transferred elsewhere.

This website does not use cookies or tracking tools.
No browsing data, analytics, or background information is collected.

6. Your Rights

Since we only use your WhatsApp number for communication, you can request at any time that we delete or stop using your number.
Simply send a message on WhatsApp requesting deletion.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if our data collection practices change.
All updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.
